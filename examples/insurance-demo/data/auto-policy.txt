Coverage Summary

This auto insurance policy provides comprehensive coverage for personal vehicles. The policy covers bodily injury liability up to $250,000 per person and $500,000 per accident. Property damage liability is covered up to $100,000 per accident.

Collision Coverage

Collision coverage pays for damage to your vehicle resulting from a collision with another vehicle or object, regardless of fault. The deductible is $500 per incident. Coverage applies to the listed vehicle and any rental vehicle used as a temporary replacement.

Comprehensive Coverage

Comprehensive coverage protects against non-collision losses including theft, vandalism, natural disasters, falling objects, and animal strikes. The deductible is $250. Glass damage claims may be processed with no deductible in participating states.

Exclusions

The following are excluded from coverage: intentional damage by the policyholder, damage while the vehicle is used for commercial purposes or ride-sharing without a commercial endorsement, racing or speed contests, damage caused while operating under the influence of alcohol or drugs, wear and tear, and mechanical breakdown.

Claims Process

To file a claim, contact our 24/7 claims center at 1-800-555-CLAIM. Provide your policy number, date of incident, description of damage, and a police report number if applicable. An adjuster will be assigned within 48 hours. Claims must be filed within 30 days of the incident.

Pricing

The annual premium for this policy is $1,200. A 10% discount applies for safe driver records, and a 5% multi-policy discount is available when bundled with home insurance. Payment plans are available quarterly or monthly with a $3 monthly service fee.
