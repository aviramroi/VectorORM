Coverage Summary

This term life insurance policy provides a death benefit of $500,000 for a term of 20 years. The policy covers the named insured and pays the designated beneficiary upon the death of the insured during the policy term.

Death Benefit

The full death benefit of $500,000 is payable to the named beneficiary upon the death of the insured from any cause, subject to the exclusions listed below. The benefit is paid as a lump sum unless an alternative settlement option is elected.

Convertibility

This term policy may be converted to a permanent whole life policy without additional medical underwriting during the first 15 years of the term. The premium for the converted policy will be based on the insured's attained age at the time of conversion.

Accelerated Death Benefit

If the insured is diagnosed with a terminal illness with a life expectancy of 12 months or less, up to 50% of the death benefit may be accessed early. The accelerated amount is discounted by 8% and reduces the remaining death benefit accordingly.

Exclusions

The following are excluded from coverage: death by suicide within the first two years of the policy (contestability period), death resulting from participation in illegal activities, death caused by acts of war or military service in a combat zone, and material misrepresentation on the application.

Claims Process

Beneficiaries should contact 1-800-555-LIFE to initiate a claim. Required documentation includes a certified death certificate, completed claim form, and proof of beneficiary identity. Claims are typically processed within 30 business days of receiving complete documentation.

Pricing

The annual premium is $480 for a healthy non-smoking male aged 35. Premiums are guaranteed level for the full 20-year term. A waiver of premium rider is available for an additional $60 per year, which waives premiums if the insured becomes totally disabled.
