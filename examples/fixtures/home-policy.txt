Coverage Summary

This homeowners insurance policy provides protection for your dwelling, personal property, and liability. The dwelling is insured for replacement cost up to $350,000. Personal property coverage is set at 70% of the dwelling amount.

Dwelling Coverage

Dwelling coverage pays to repair or rebuild your home if it is damaged by a covered peril. Covered perils include fire, lightning, windstorm, hail, explosion, smoke damage, vandalism, and theft. The dwelling deductible is $1,000.

Personal Property

Personal property coverage protects your belongings inside and outside the home. High-value items such as jewelry over $2,500, art over $5,000, and electronics over $3,000 require scheduled personal property endorsements for full replacement value coverage.

Liability Coverage

Personal liability coverage provides up to $300,000 in protection if you are found legally responsible for bodily injury or property damage to others. Medical payments to others coverage provides up to $5,000 per person for injuries on your property regardless of fault.

Exclusions

The following are excluded from coverage: flood damage (requires separate flood policy), earthquake damage, damage from pests or vermin, mold resulting from neglect, normal wear and deterioration, government action or war, nuclear hazard, and intentional loss or damage by the policyholder.

Claims Process

Report claims by calling 1-800-555-HOME or through the online portal. Provide photographs of damage, a detailed inventory of damaged items with approximate values, and receipts where available. Emergency repairs to prevent further damage are covered up to $5,000 without prior approval.

Pricing

The annual premium is $1,800. A 15% discount applies for homes with monitored security systems. A 5% discount is available for claims-free policyholders with five or more consecutive years. Bundling with auto insurance provides an additional 5% discount.
